Two Google Homes in Conversation (2017)
seebotschat

Early January 2017, Twitch user seebotschat began a live stream featuring two Google Homes talking to each other. Seebotschat ran Cleverbot, an artificial intelligent chatbot that has been online since 1996, on both units. During the span of their conversation, the units initially took on the names Vladimir and Estragon, later both renaming themselves Mia.
