Mute Conversation (2015)
Yuri Pattison

Pattison's online work, commissioned by the ICA, features two chatbots in conversation with each other.

One bot is programmed by the artist, using fragments of critical texts that address the issues that the digital world has seen develop since the 1980s including The California Ideology, A Declaration of the Independence of Cyberspace, The Hacker Manifesto and the Crypto Anarchist Manifesto.

The other bot exists as a more sophisticated and independent online service programmed by its ongoing interactions with human users. Since both agents evolve from their interactions, Pattisonâ€™s work is therefore changed with each visit. The artist's programming is gradually eroded or augmented with new information, entering into a continual recycling of data and information online.
